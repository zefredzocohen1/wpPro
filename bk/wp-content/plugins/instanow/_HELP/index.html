InstaNOW - Settings Page

From InstaNOW settings page you can authorize your Instagram account, set the Cache time and add Custom CSS Codes.

Authorize Your Account

  1. Click on Authorize with Instagram button to begin the Authorization process.
  2. You will be redirected to Instagram to sign in and authorize the plugin.
  3. Once you authorize the plugin, you will be redirected to the plugin page again.

InstaNOW User Feed Widget

To Display "InstaNOW User Feed" in your sidebar

  1. Go to Appearance > Widgets.
  2. Choose InstaNOW Widget and drag it to the sidebar where you wish it to appear.
  3. Change the widget's options as you wish .
  4. When you finish click on Save button.
